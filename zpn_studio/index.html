About Me

Hi, I'm Zach Newby. I am a computer science student from Montana and Missouri who likes to draw, write, create, and make people happy. I created this website for my Web Design Fundamentals class as a place to share my creations. I like to share my artwork so this website will be an online gallery for all to view them on. I also want to make comics, short stories, and maybe blog posts, but currently they are not featured here.
