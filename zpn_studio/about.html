About Zach's Studio

This is a website where I can share my drawings, paintings, and ideas. I made it for my BYU Idaho Online Web Fundamentals Class (WDD130). Later on I may add in stories and blogposts, or keep those on a seperate site for convience. I will try to add new images now and then, so long as it is feasible. In the future I may may feature comics, blogposts, and short stories, or I may just host them on a seperate site with links. I hope you enjoy my creations.

My forte is drawing, but I have considered branching out to dioramas. For digital artwork, I use a Wacom Intuos and my preferred art software is Clip Studio Paint Pro. As for paper, I usually use a 4h pencil, 4B for shading, and fineliner pens for inking.
