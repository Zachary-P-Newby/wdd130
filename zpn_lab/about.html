About Zach's Labrtory

This is a website where I can share my art, stories, ideas, and if I really want, blogposts. It is a library, art gallery, and blog all in one, hence the title of Zach's Labratory. (L.A.B.) In a way. Not to mention where do you usually conduct expiriments? in a laboratory.

My forte is drawing, but I have considered branching out to dioramas. For digital artwork, I use an Wacom Intuos and my preferred art software is Cilp Studio Paint Pro. As for paper, usually a 4h pencil, 4B for shading, and fineliner pens for inking.
